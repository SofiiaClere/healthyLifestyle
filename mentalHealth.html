Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.

When we are mentally healthy, we enjoy our life and environment, and the people in it.

We can be creative, learn, try new things, and take risks. We are better able to cope with difficult times in our personal and professional lives.
